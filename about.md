# About the Science Hub Dashboard 🌍

Welcome to the centralised knowledge and results dashboard for the **ESA Earth System Science Hub**, seamlessly integrated within the **EarthCODE** environment. 

::: tip Our Mission
To synthesise, visualise, and track the diverse research findings, algorithms, and collaborative scientific outputs emerging from the ESA Science Hub community into a single, interactive platform.
:::

## What is the ESA Science Hub?

The **Earth System Science Hub** is the European Space Agency's premier facility for advanced Earth system and climate science studies. Located at ESA ESRIN in Frascati, Italy, it serves as a dynamic centre of excellence designed for:

- **Scientific Collaboration:** Fostering a community response to the major environmental and climate science challenges of this decade.
- **Networking & Exchange:** Welcoming scientists from all domains of Earth Observation (EO) and Earth system science to share ideas and methodologies.
- **Boosting Output:** Enhancing the scientific capabilities of ESA and its Member States by providing direct access to ESA's world-class expertise and infrastructure.

## Highlighted Research Areas

The Science Hub continually generates cutting-edge insights. By exploring this dashboard, you can track the results of major community projects, such as:

::: info 🌾 AI for Near Real-Time Crop Monitoring
Developing new transformer-based methods to enhance Green Area Index (GAI) retrieval using **Sentinel-1** and **Sentinel-2** data. This research delivers reliable, all-weather crop monitoring in near real-time.
:::

::: info 🌲 European Forest Resilience
Studies led by ESRIN Internal Research Fellows that explore the relationship between forest structural diversity (FSD) and canopy structural complexity to better understand ecosystem resilience.
:::

::: info 🏙️ Atmospheric & Climate Probing
Tracking outcomes from key initiatives like the **BAQUNIN project** (probing the urban atmosphere from the ground) and advancements in spaceborne Doppler velocity measurements.
:::
